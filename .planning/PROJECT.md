# Land Kings — Project Brief

## What This Is

A single-page marketing website for Land Kings, a land investing company operating in Texas and Louisiana. The site serves three distinct audiences and drives specific actions from each.

## Core Value

A professional, trust-building presence that converts landowners, deal sources, and investors into qualified contacts — with zero friction from awareness to submission.

## Audience Definitions

### 1. Land Sellers (Primary)
Owners of rural vacant land (5+ acres) looking to liquidate. Often inherited property, retired landowners, or investors divesting.
**Primary action:** Submit contact form with property info.

### 2. Birddogs / Brokers / Deal Sources
Professionals or freelancers who find land deals and get referral fees. Need to submit structured deal info (acreage, APN, utilities, access, etc.).
**Primary action:** Submit deal submission form with property details.

### 3. Land Investors
Buyers looking for raw land to purchase — buy-and-hold, development plays, portfolio accumulation.
**Primary action:** Submit investor inquiry form.

## Site Structure (Single Page, Section-Based)

1. **Hero** — Brand statement, value prop, primary CTA
2. **About / Who We Are** — Company story, credibility, specialization (large acreage, subdivisions, development)
3. **What We Buy** — Detailed property types (large acreage, subdivisions, development land)
4. **How It Works** — Simple 3-4 step process for sellers
5. **For Deal Sources** — Birddog/broker section with referral program explanation
6. **For Investors** — Investor section with acquisition criteria
7. **Contact** — Three forms (sellers, deal sources, investors) or inline section links
8. **Footer** — Company info, disclaimers

## Differentiation Angles

- **Large acreage specialization** — NOT typical 1-5 acre lot buyers
- **Subdivisions** — Can handle partitioned land, not just raw raw land
- **Development deals** — Corporate/developer connections, off-market opportunities
- **Speed + certainty** — Close quickly, cash offers, no financing contingencies
- **Texas + Louisiana expertise** — Local market knowledge, local relationships
