# Land Kings — Project Brief

## What This Is

A single-page marketing website for Land Kings, a land investing company operating in Texas and Louisiana. The site serves three distinct audiences and drives specific actions from each.

## Core Value

A professional, trust-building presence that converts landowners, deal sources, and investors into qualified contacts — with zero friction from awareness to submission.

## Key Decisions

| Decision | Rationale | Outcome |
|----------|-----------|---------|
| Single-page layout | Fast navigation, mobile-friendly, lower development cost | Active |
| Three separate contact forms | Each audience has distinct info needs; tailored forms = better lead quality | Active |
| Large acreage + subdivision focus | Differentiates from commodity "we buy any land" competitors | Active |
| Texas Triangle + Lubbock County | Geographic focus (I-35/I-10/I-45 corridor + Lubbock) | Active |
| Static HTML/CSS/JS | Simple hosting, fast load, no backend needed for lead capture | Active |
| 48-hour response commitment | Promised on all forms | Active |
| Formspree → offers@landkings.biz | All forms route here | Active |

## Operating Geography

- **Primary:** Texas Triangle (I-35, I-10, I-45 corridor)
- **Secondary:** Lubbock County
- Also covers surrounding counties in these regions

## Audience Definitions

### 1. Land Sellers (Primary)
Owners of rural vacant land (5+ acres) looking to liquidate. Often inherited property, retired landowners, or investors divesting.
**Primary action:** Submit seller contact form.

### 2. Birddogs / Brokers / Deal Sources
Professionals who find land deals and get referral fees. Commission structure: **10% of gross sale price, paid at closing** (per Bird Dog Agreement).
**Primary action:** Submit deal info via two-tier form.

### 3. Land Investors
Buyers looking for raw land — buy-and-hold, development plays, portfolio accumulation.
**Primary action:** Submit investor inquiry form.

## Site Structure (Single Page, Section-Based)

1. **Hero** — "Claim Your Crown. Build a Land Legacy." + trust signals
2. **About** — "Built by Operators, Not Theorists" + vendor bench
3. **What We Buy** — Large acreage, subdivisions, development deals, TX Triangle + Lubbock County
4. **How It Works** — Submit → Review → Two Offers → Close (15-30 business days cash)
5. **For Sellers** — Dual-offer method (Cash + seller-finance), title/survey handled
6. **For Birddogs/Brokers** — "Bring Us the Hairy Ones", commission protection, 10% referral fee
7. **For Investors** — "How We De-Risk Dirt", milestone funding, quarterly reporting
8. **FAQ** — Speed, liens/taxes, landlocked, out-of-state, guarantees
9. **Footer** — Contact, disclaimers

## Key Copy Themes (from source docs)

- **Access first** — "We operate where people, pavement, and policy align"
- **Dual offers** — Cash + seller-finance in writing, plain English
- **Entitlement handled** — Survey, access permits, utility letters/CCNs, drainage concepts
- **Builder underwriting** — Frontage/access, utility feasibility, soils/drainage, tract sizes
- **Milestone funding** — Budget-gated releases, multiple exits (wholesale, paper lots, vertical)
- **Transparency** — "We price deals the way builders buy, and we publish timelines like adults"

## Differentiation Angles

- **Large acreage + subdivision** — NOT typical 1-5 acre lot buyers
- **Development deals** — Corporate/developer connections, off-market opportunities
- **Dual-offer method** — Cash vs seller-finance, seller chooses
- **Access + entitlement expertise** — Solves "hairy" deals others won't touch
- **Speed** — 15-30 business days cash, milestone timelines for entitlement deals
- **Texas Triangle expertise** — I-35/I-10/I-45 corridor knowledge + Lubbock County

## Birddog/Broker Program

- Commission: **10% of gross sale price**, paid at closing
- Commission protection: "Your relationship, protected"
- Fast ICs (Independent Contractor agreements)
- "Access fixes, easement gaps, partial utilities, flood fringe — we provide an entitlement plan you can present"
