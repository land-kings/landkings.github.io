Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 16, 2026

Information We Collect

When you submit a contact form on our website, we collect the following information:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Property details including state, county, acreage, address, and estimated value
  • Any additional information you voluntarily provide in your message

How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected through our forms to:

  • Evaluate your property and provide a response within 48 hours
  • Communicate with you about your inquiry or submitted deal
  • Process transactions if we move forward with a purchase
  • Improve our service and website

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your information is used solely for communication between you and Land Kings LLC regarding your inquiry.

We may share information with service providers who assist us in operating our website and business, such as our email service provider (Formspree), but only as necessary to process your submissions.

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Cookies

Our website may use basic cookies to improve user experience. You can control cookie settings in your browser.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal information
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Opt out of communications at any time by contacting us at offers@landkings.biz
